Threat Awareness

Hostile Reconnaissance

Identifying, recording and reporting suspicious behaviour
⚠ Life-threatening situations — call Triple Zero (000) immediately
You know what's normal in your everyday work environment — trust your instincts. Activities or possessions that appear out of context may be an indicator of hostile reconnaissance. Suspicious behaviour or activity should be reported to authorities.
Recognition & Recording

🔎 What is Suspicious Behaviour?

  • 👁️
    Unusual building surveillance such as noting exits and security in high patronage areas
  • 📹
    Unusual interest in security measures and routines, including over-inquisitive, unusual or persistent questions
  • 🔄
    Recurring visitors or loiterers with weak reasoning for their presence on site
  • 😰
    Anxious or nervous behaviour such as clock watching, shaking or pacing
  • 🕵️
    Attempting to remain inconspicuous, or leaving the area if noticed or avoiding security and CCTV
  • 🚪
    Attempting to gain illegitimate access to premises — impersonating staff, tailgating through controlled areas
  • 🚗
    Suspicious vehicle activity in close proximity to the site

📋 What to Record

  • ✓
    Time, date and place
  • ✓
    Description of the person and clothing
  • ✓
    What activity or suspicious behaviour the person was displaying
  • ✓
    What was their reaction to you observing or speaking to them?
  • ✓
    How did they enter and exit your site?
  • ✓
    Vehicle description, location and registration details
  • ✓
    Activities or items that person may have had a specific interest in
  • ✓
    Were they captured on CCTV? Specify location
